Starbucks has been an inspiration in my work ethics, GIT degree, and even some of my projects. During Fall 2020 there was a mini Starbucks Case Study assigned to help receive a better understanding of their enterprise.

The following year Fall 2021 Starbucks celebrated their 50th anniversary and allowed me to use my knowledge of digital illustration and publishing to create a celebratory poster based on the Starbucks fonts, logos, and colors over the last 50 years.
